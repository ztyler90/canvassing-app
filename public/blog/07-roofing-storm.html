Roofing Field Guide

After the Storm:
How Roofing Crews Can Canvas Faster and Lose Fewer Leads

Storm season is a sprint. The teams that win aren't the biggest — they're the ones who move fastest without dropping leads.

Urgent
The First 48 Hours

What you need ready before the storm hits — because once it does, there's no time to set up.

🗺️

A Map You Can Mark Up Quickly

Drop pins on affected neighborhoods within hours. Assign territories before reps hit the street.

📝

A Tight Rep Script

Storm canvassing is sensitive. Reps need a clear, empathetic approach that opens doors, not slams them.

📋

A Standard Intake Form

Capture homeowner info, damage notes, insurance carrier, and preferred callback time — consistently, every door.

The Long Game
Every Door Is a Data Point

No-answers and soft nos aren't dead leads.

After a storm, homeowners are overwhelmed. A no-answer on day 2 might be a yes on day 14. A soft no ("we're still figuring it out") is a follow-up opportunity in 2-4 weeks. Track every outcome so your team can circle back at the right time instead of guessing who to revisit.

Best Practices
The Insurance Conversation
🛤️

Stay in Your Lane

Reps are not insurance adjusters. Don't make claims about coverage or promise what insurance will pay.

📅

Book the Inspection, Not the Sale

The goal at the door is a free roof inspection appointment. The sale happens after the assessment, not before.

📌

Capture Every Promise

If a rep tells a homeowner "we'll call Tuesday," that needs to be in the system — not in the rep's head.

📄

Give Customers a Clear Process

Leave behind a simple one-pager: here's what happens next, here's who calls you, here's the timeline.

Operations
Running Multiple Neighborhoods
🔲

Territories with Clear Boundaries

When 15 reps are canvassing 5 neighborhoods, overlaps cost you credibility. Draw the lines before they leave.

👁️

Visible Coverage

Managers need to see, in real time, which streets have been worked and which haven't. No end-of-day guessing.

📆

Centralized Scheduling

Inspection appointments from 5 neighborhoods need to land in one calendar. Avoid double-booking your inspection crew.

📊

Current Status on Every Lead

Knocked, inspected, claim filed, approved, scheduled — know where every homeowner stands without calling 5 reps.

Year-Round
Off-Storm Strategy

Storms are the accelerator. Steady canvassing is the engine.

The best roofing canvassing operations don't shut down between storms. Aging roofs, visible wear, and proactive inspections keep the pipeline full even in quiet months. The data you capture during storm season — neighborhood maps, conversion patterns, seasonal trends — becomes your playbook for the rest of the year.

Teams that only canvas after storms are always reacting. Teams that canvas year-round are building a business.
