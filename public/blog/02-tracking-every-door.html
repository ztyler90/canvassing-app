The Hard Truth
Why Spreadsheets Don't Hold Up

Tired reps won't do data entry.

After 6 hours in the sun, nobody is going back to the office to type door outcomes into a spreadsheet. And even if they did, the data is stale by the time it's entered. The only system that works is one that captures data in the moment — with a single tap, while the rep is still standing on the porch.
