The Essentials
4 Things Software Must Do

Track Every Door

Every knock, every outcome, every follow-up — captured without slowing reps down.

🗺️

Manage Territories

Clear boundaries so reps aren't stepping on each other and neighborhoods aren't missed.

Surface What's Working

Real patterns — which areas convert, which times hit, which reps need help.

Watch Out
Red Flags When Evaluating
⚠️

Long Contracts Only

If they won't let you go monthly, ask why.

⚠️

Hidden Pricing

"Contact us for pricing" usually means it's more than you'd agree to upfront.

⚠️

Short or Restricted Trials

7 days isn't enough. You need a full week of real field use.

⚠️

Onboarding Upcharges

If the product needs paid onboarding, the product might be the problem.

⚠️

Summer Outages

Peak season reliability isn't optional. Check their track record.

Your Playbook
How to Choose
1

Write Down Your 4 Numbers

Doors knocked, contact rate, booking rate, close rate. Know your baseline.

2

List 3 Biggest Pains

What's actually costing you deals today? Be specific.

3

Trial with Real Reps

Not managers — the people who will use it 8 hours a day in the heat.

4

Check Post-Sale Support

What happens when something breaks on a Saturday in July?

5

Pick the Simplest That Solves Your Pains

More features ≠ better. The best tool is the one your team actually uses.
