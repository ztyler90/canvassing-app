Quality Check
Knock Count Isn't Enough

⚠️ Watch for Speed-Knocking

A rep who hits 80 doors but has 2 conversations isn't outperforming — they're skimming. Pair knock goals with quality metrics: conversations per knock, bookings per conversation, and sit rates. The number of doors only matters if the conversations are real.

The Trap
What "Until Dark" Costs You

⏰ Time-Based Goals

Rewards presence. Reps learn to look busy until the clock runs out. Slow-knockers survive. Top performers get frustrated watching others coast.

🎯 Number-Based Goals

Rewards productivity. Reps who hit their number earn their evening back. Efficient reps thrive. Accountability is clear and measurable.

Adjustments
Territory Adjustment + Recovery Rhythm
🗺️

Territory Adjustment

Not all turf is equal. Adjust daily targets based on density, drive time, and territory quality. A rural area with spread-out homes needs a different number than a dense suburban block.

🔄

Recovery Rhythm

Bad days happen. Build in a weekly recovery mechanism — if a rep misses Monday's target, what does the rest of the week look like? Define a plan, not just pressure.

Habits
Make Goals Visible
🌅

State Every Morning

Say the number out loud. Every rep should know their target before they leave the office.

📱

Progress Visible All Day

Reps should be able to see where they stand at any moment — not guess.

🌙

End-of-Day Check-Ins

Quick daily review: did we hit it? What got in the way? Keep it short and constructive.

📅

Weekly Number Review

Zoom out once a week. Patterns show up in weekly data that daily noise hides.
