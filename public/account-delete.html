Delete your KnockIQ account

You can permanently delete your KnockIQ account and the personal data we hold about you at any time. There are two ways: directly inside the app, or by emailing us. Both result in the same outcome.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

AIf you're using the iPhone, iPad, or Android app

  1. Open KnockIQ and sign in.
  2. Tap the Settings (gear) icon in the top-right header.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen and find Account.
  4. Tap Delete my account… in the red "Delete my account" card.
  5. Type DELETE in the confirmation field and tap Permanently delete my account.

Your session ends immediately. The deletion is processed right then — no waiting.

BIf you're using KnockIQ on the web

  1. Sign in at app.getknockiq.com.
  2. Click the Settings icon in the top-right header.
  3. Scroll to the Account section.
  4. Click Delete my account… and follow the confirmation prompt.

Option 2 — Email us

If you've lost access to your account or prefer to request deletion by email, send a request to the address below from the email address you registered with. Include the words "Delete my account" in the subject line so we route it correctly.

privacy@knockiq.com

We respond to all deletion requests within 72 hours on business days and complete the deletion within 30 days at the latest (most are processed the same day). We will email you a confirmation once the deletion is complete.

What gets deleted

Permanently removed:
  • Your name, email address, phone number, and avatar photo
  • Your authentication record (you can no longer sign in)
  • Your personal stats, commission history, and notification settings
  • Any draft content or settings tied to you personally

What gets kept (and why)

Retained on the team's records:
  • Sessions and interactions you logged while working — door knocks, conversations, appointments, bookings, photos attached to leads. These belong to the canvassing company that employed you, not to KnockIQ. They are detached from your name and kept on the team's own records for their reporting, accounting, and legal needs.
  • Aggregated, de-identified metrics — anonymized counts used for product analytics and statistical reporting.

If you also want the data above removed, contact your team's owner directly (they control the company's records under our terms), or contact us if your team no longer exists.

Special case: organization owners

If you're the owner of a KnockIQ team (the person who created the company account and pays the subscription), deleting your account also deletes the entire organization, every teammate's account, and every record on the team's data. KnockIQ teams have exactly one owner; the owner leaving means the team is gone. The app will warn you about this before you confirm.

Need help?

If you have trouble with any of the steps above, reach us at hello@knockiq.com. We're happy to help.
