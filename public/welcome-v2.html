◆ Pipeline

Built to run the whole sales cycle — not just track it.

From the moment a rep knocks a door to the moment the deal is signed, KnockIQ's pipeline keeps every lead, every closer, and every appointment moving — whether you set appointments for next Tuesday or close at the door.

  • 📅
    Appointment setting Setters mark a lead as interested and the calendar/closer hand-off happens automatically — no texts, no shared spreadsheets.
  • 🤝
    Assigning leads to closers Route by territory, by setter, or round-robin. Closers get notified the second a qualified lead lands in their queue.
  • 🛡️
    Zero dropped leads Stale-deal alerts, follow-up reminders, and lead-aging surfaced on the board mean nothing rots in a phase.
  • 🔁
    Manage the full sales cycle Custom phases, required fields per phase, and conversion at every step — so you see exactly where leads stall and where dollars come from.
  • ⚙️
    Configured for how your team sells Appointment-setting model or book-at-the-door model — both first-class. Switch motions per crew, per region, or per campaign.

◆ Territory management

Draw it. Assign it. Own the street.

Carve up neighborhoods on a map, assign reps in two clicks, and stop reps from stepping on each other — or knocking where they shouldn't.

  • ✎
    Draw territories on the map Free-hand polygons, named, colored, sized to what your crew can cover in a shift.
  • 👥
    Assign in two clicks One rep, multiple reps, or rotate weekly. Reassign as fast as the schedule changes.
  • 🚫
    DNK zones, baked in Layer do-not-knock polygons over any territory and the rep app silently suppresses logs inside them.
  • 📍
    Live view of who's where The same map shows active reps in real time — so you always know whose street is being worked.
