One platform

Everything else you'd expect —
and a few things you wouldn't.

Under the hood: smart door detection, offline-first sync, and the small details that make the difference between "app they tolerate" and "app they open first thing."

🛰️

Smart door detection

GPS + phone motion classifier distinguishes a real knock from a drive-by. Adaptive polling dials accuracy up or down based on whether you're walking or stopped.

🧭

Territory & boundaries

Draw polygon territories, assign reps, and overlay do-not-knock zones. The detector suppresses knocks inside DNKs automatically.

🎯

Goal ring + confetti

Daily goals by revenue or estimate count, set per-org. Hit it and the session-complete screen celebrates with animation and haptics.

🏅

Gold / silver / bronze

The leaderboard is real-time and uses gold/silver/bronze medals. Reps see their movement week-over-week — with the callout to match.

⚡

Offline-first PWA

Log interactions with no signal — everything syncs when the phone comes back online. Nothing's ever lost in a dead zone.

🔒

RLS-scoped by org

Built on Supabase with row-level security. Your reps only see their own data; your managers only see their org. No data crossover.
